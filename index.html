What is the meaning of a Bank Statement?

A bank statement or account statement is a document issued by a bank describing the activities in a depositor’s savings account or current account during the period. Account holders can keep a track of their transactions and account through the bank statements.

What is included in an Account Statement?

A bank statement typically provides a full overview of the account for the selected tenure. Account statement summarizes the following:

    • The beginning and ending balances of the account
    • Deposits in the form of income, cash deposits, etc.
    • Interest earned on the account
    • Service fees and penalties charged against the account
    • Funds withdrawn from the account in the form of cash, cheques, etc.

What is the Bank Statement format?

An account statement usually contains three parts:

  • Account holder Details
  • Account Details
  • Transaction history

The account statement begins with the account holder's information provided at the top, which consists of the Name, registered mobile number, and residential address.

Following the account holder details, an account summary is presented, containing the account number, home address, account description (type of account), and other relevant information about the depositor.

The transaction detail section displays a comprehensive record of all transactions, including the date, amount, and a description of the payee or payer.

A customer has the option to choose the statement period for which he/she wants the bank statement.

For eg: A customer can subscribe to get the account statement for the complete financial year or choose to get the account statement for the statement period of last three months or any specific range of days.

Different banks offer different statement periods hence a customer can choose the statement period according to his/her requirement.

How to get a Bank Statement?

There are two ways by which a bank statement can be availed:

  • Online Mode
  • Offline Mode

How to get Bank Statement online?

Online mode refers to the electronic version or paperless mode where account holders can access their statements, known as e-statements, online through mobile banking apps or netbanking portals, and download them as PDFs.

In certain cases, when accessing statements through mobile banking apps or netbanking accounts, some banks do not offer the option to download or print the bank statements. In such situations, banks send statements via email to customers, which can be downloaded or printed.

Some banks automatically send monthly statements by mail, which will be identical to the online version. To ensure security, these mailed statements are password protected and can only be accessed by providing the correct credentials.

Here are some of the steps to access the bank statement online:

Step 1: Login to the account through the bank’s net banking portal or mobile banking app.

Step 2: Look for the heading “bank statement” or “e-statements”.

Step 3: Select the statement period.

Step 4: The bank will send the account statement for the selected period on the registered email ID of the account holder. Alternatively, some banks also provide the option of downloading the bank statement as a PDF through the netbanking portal or mobile banking app itself.

How to get Bank Statement offline?

In offline mode, bank statements are typically sent out via post by some banks.For most banks customer needs to visit the bank branch and fill in the bank statement request letter to collect the bank reconciliation statement.

Different banks offer different statement periods hence a customer can choose the statement period according to his/her requirement and collect the same from the bank branch.

ATMs also offer the option to print a summarized version of a bank statement, called a transaction history. Hence, one can opt for the ATM option too.

What one should prefer?

Nowadays, the majority of banks offer the option to access bank statements online, providing customers with an easier way to review their transaction history and maintain a record of their expenses. Undoubtedly, online access to bank statements is convenient, readily available, and free of charge. However, there are a few situations where it is not sufficient.

In cases like filing a tax return, customer needs to get the certified bank statement, which will be considered as a proof for financial standing. Banks only provide certified account statements via offline mode. Thus, account holders need to visit the nearby bank branch to avail certified account statements. Charges of such stamped statements varies from one bank to another and bank deduct the same from the savings / current account.

Hence, under such scenarios it becomes essential for the account holders to visit the bank branch and get account statement offline.

What is the difference between a Bank Statement and Mini Statement?

Bank Statement Mini Statement
A bank statement is a list of all transactions for a bank account over a set period, usually monthly / year. A mini statement is a list of recent 3 or 5 transactions carried out by a customer.
Shows transaction details for the statement period requested by the customer Displays last 3 or 5 transaction details, depending upon the bank.
Accessing an  account statement online is free of cost but accessing certified account statement by visiting the bank is chargable. Accessing a mini statement is free of cost.

What can be the alterative for a bank statement?

Editable bank statement examples are digital templates that allow users to customize and modify the details on a bank statement to suit their specific needs. These templates are commonly used for various purposes, such as creating proof of income or expense reports for personal or business use.

One of the key advantages of editable bank statement examples is their flexibility. Users can input their own financial information, including transaction details, balances, and account numbers, making it appear as if it were an authentic bank statement. This feature is particularly useful for individuals who may need to present financial documentation for specific applications, such as loan approvals, rental applications, or visa applications.

It's important to note that while editable bank statement examples can be helpful in certain situations, they should only be used ethically and responsibly. Falsifying or altering financial documents with the intent to deceive or commit fraud is illegal and can lead to severe consequences.

When using editable bank statement examples, it is crucial to ensure that they are used for legitimate purposes and comply with any legal and ethical requirements. They should only be used as a template or a guide to create accurate representations of actual financial transactions.

What are the features of an Account Statement?

Monitor account balance 

A bank statement helps a customer to keep a track on his/her expenses and transactions (debit and credit both). Hence, a customer becomes aware and manages his/her finance accordingly.

Fee and Interest Tracking

If a bank or credit union gives interest, one can see how much money is being made on their savings every month. Depending on the interest earned, one may put some of the money in an investment account or money market to earn more.

Identify fraud 

A bank statement allows one to review his/her statements regularly to help spot fraudulent activity, like someone else using the debit card etc.

FAQs

Q. What is the significance of Electronic statements?

A. To reduce the cost of postage and the generation of paper account statements, some banks encourage their users to receive account statements electronically. Hence, e-statements are sent out via email and can be further viewed and downloaded by the customer. Availing the e-statement is much more convenient and is free of cost.

Q. How to request for bank statement offline?

A. Customer needs to visit the bank branch to avail bank statement offline. The customer might be required to fill in the bank statement application form to collect his / her statement.

Q. How to download bank statement online?

A. Most banks send a monthly account statement if the customer has registered their email address with them. All account statements sent through emails are password protected hence make sure to read the password instructions in the email to download bank statement PDF.
